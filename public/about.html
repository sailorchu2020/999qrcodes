About me
I'm Q, a 17-year-old high school girl from California hoping to learn more about the wonders of programming and graphic design. Coding has been an interest of mine for a couple of years now, and I plan to pursue it professionally in the future through frontend development or software engineering. Truthfully, coding is something that's quite intimidating for me, but I hope to reduce that feeling each time I open VS Code to update this site! I'm highly inexperienced when it comes to having a good website, so please feel free to contact me if you have any suggestions or tips about making this be the best it can possibly be! Other than that, I intend to use this site as a way to express my interests and show off stuff that I've made to anyone who cares enough about it. Currently, there are still several pages that I plan to add in the future, so stay tuned for them!
About the site
This site was first created on November 6, 2023. I heard about Neocities through the YouTube video Hey Peebrain, You Teleport? Tumblr's Dark Mystery from Izzzyzzz. This video made a huge impression on me, and it pushed me to create my own site on Neocities, albeit not an angelic ARG. I tried learning HTML/CSS for a little while until I completely lost interest... Fast forward to 2025, where I'm considering following a career in the world of computer science. I recalled this little place I had going on in Neocities. So, here we are! My goal is to make this website a project that I can be proud of, and to have something that I can consistently update for fun.
